Achievements

Venkateshwarians Shine at the Annual Interschool Fest

Budding talents of VGS participated in the annual Interschool Fest ‘The Quintessence’ organized by Brain International School On November 21, 2020.The event offered a gamut of competitions ranging from artistic skills to technological prowess that provided an engaging platform to unleash the talent of students. Samridh Gupta (Preschool-Phoenix) secured Third position and Devanshi Kochhar (class1-Orion) secured First position. Heartiest Congratulations to the winners!

The VGS bags the STEAM Education Excellence Award!

The VGS bags the STEAM Education Excellence Award! Venkateshwar Global School was awarded the ‘STEAM Education Excellence’ by Education World; making it rank as the 3rd Best School in the City and 8th Best School in the country! The school firmly believes that STEAM activities should be a pivotal part of the curriculum to encourage students’ creative and critical thinking skills. Our students have proved their mettle in all fields be it in IRC League, Science, Mathematics, Information Technology etc. It is truly a moment of pride and honour for all!
